About

Matthew Phillips is a cellist and string arranger. Based in Liverpool, he studied cello with Uli Heinen at Royal Birmingham Conservatoire (1st Class Honours). He’s written and recorded on works for Take That, Michael Head & The Red Elastic Band, The K’s, Andrew Cushin, Crawlers, The Fernweh, credits, and The Wood. Most recently, his arrangements have been featured on long-time collaborator Astles’ debut album ‘Soundtrack for the 21 Bus Home’. He has recorded samples for Fracture Sounds, and is featured as a soloist on their ‘Soft Strings’ and ‘String Formations’ libraries.
